#### Tracking Technologies and Cookies

The Company uses Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on Our Service and store certain information. Tracking technologies used are short links, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze Our Service. The technologies The Company uses may include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies.** A cookie is a small file placed on Your Device. Some parts of our Service require cookies to be stored on Your Device. If You have adjusted Your browser settings so that it will refuse Cookies, our Service may not function as intended.
- **Short Links.** The Company may use short links in our communications to track clicks and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns. These links may redirect through a tracking service before reaching the final destination. Currently, The Company hosts a short link service and does not sell or disclose click data to Third Parties or Affiliates.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies remain on Your personal computer or mobile device when You go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted as soon as You close Your web browser.

The Company use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes set out below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**

  Type: Session Cookies

  Administered by: The Company

  Purpose: These Cookies are essential to provide You with services available through the Website and to enable You to use some of its features. They help to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services that You have asked for cannot be provided, and The Company only use these Cookies to provide You with those services. Such as the contact form.

- **Functionality Cookies**

  Type: Persistent Cookies

  Administered by: The Company

  Purpose: These Cookies allow us to remember choices You make when You use the Website, such as remembering your login details or language preference. The purpose of these Cookies is to provide You with a more personal experience and to avoid You having to re-enter your preferences every time You use the Website or Service.

- **Right to Know:** You have the right to request information about the personal information The Company collects, uses, discloses, and sells.
- **Right to Delete:** You can request deletion of personal information The Company has collected about you, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Opt-Out:** If The Company sells personal information, you have the right to opt-out of that sale. Please note that The Company currently does not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA to any Third Party or Affiliate.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** The Company will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise your rights under CCPA, you can contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. The Company may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.
